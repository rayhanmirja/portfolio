I recently sat down to map out exactly what I want to achieve before the end of the year. Building a scalable technical business has pushed me into areas I hadn't expected—like operations, B2B sales automation, and hiring architecture. Here is what I am focused on.

### The Problem

Many new startups struggle to connect their frontend UI effectively to complex backend flows without accruing massive tech debt. I encountered this repeatedly during client work throughout 2023.

### The Goal

My main objective is scaling **Minimistic** aggressively over the next 4 months. To do this, I need to:
1. Finalize the internal tooling architecture for client-onboarding.
2. Publish at least two definitive deep-dives on my YouTube channel explaining these architectural constraints.
3. Master Next.js API endpoint streaming for LLMs.

> "The hardest thing about engineering isn't the code; it's deciding what *not* to build."

### Conclusion
By mapping this out publicly, I'm forcing myself to be accountable. I'll circle back in a few months to review what worked, what failed, and what I had to pivot away from.
